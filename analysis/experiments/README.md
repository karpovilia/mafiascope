# Experiment record behind the demo paper

Analysis reports (with raw numbers in the paired .json files), the fork
selection metadata, and the drivers for every experiment the paper and its
supplement cite: probe-vote coupling baselines, the unprobed control and the
randomized A/B, test-retest noise floor, parse-rate missingness accounting,
the move-quality quadrants, the bifurcation experiment with its random-vote
control, and the F1-F5 replication audit. Pipelines: src/bifurcation/,
src/ab_invasiveness/.
